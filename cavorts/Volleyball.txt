RULES AND REGULATIONS

•The Server must serve from behind the end line until after crossing of net.

•Maximum of three hits per side.

•Player may not hit the ball twice in succession (A block is not considered a hit)

•Team may be formed in two formats:

i) Team should have 10 players (6 Boys and 4 Girls), Out of which 6 will play in court (4 Boys and 2 Girls).

ii) Team should have 10 boys out of which 6 will play on court.
