RULES AND REGULATIONS

•Each competitor is allowed a maximum of three trials at any one height.

•A competitor is eliminated when they have had three consecutive unsuccessful trials, regardless of the height or heights at which the trials were attempted.

•Competitors may use all three trials or may elect to pass a height and take the remaining trial or trials at a subsequent height.

•A competitor may attempt to clear the bar in any manner, provided the takeoff is from one foot and provided no weights or artificial aids are used.

•All of the competitor’s body must go over the bar.
